Beef Tibs

Ingredient

  • 1 pound cubed flat-iron steak
  • ¼ cup red onion, sliced
  • 1 roma tomato, cut in wedges
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil (or preferred alternative)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon berbere
  • 2 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground black cardamom or black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Start with the sauce by adding the tomatoes and onions to a pan and cook on medium heat for 3 to 4 minutes, until soft.
  2. Then add 2 tablespoons of oil and the berbere. Turn the heat to low and simmer for about 5 minutes.
  3. Next, add the garlic and simmer for 2 more minutes. The last step for the sauce is to add ½ teaspoon of salt and the black cardamom or black pepper.
  4. In a separate pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil on medium-high heat, then add the meat.
  5. Sprinkle the meat with ½ teaspoon of salt and cook for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Move the meat to the pan with the berbere sauce. Cook until done, about 8-10 minutes.
